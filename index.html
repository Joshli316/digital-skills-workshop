在手机上设置 Gmail Set Up Gmail on Your Phone

免费 · 每位求职者都需要一个专业的电子邮件地址 Free — every job seeker needs a professional email

    下载 Gmail AppDownload the Gmail app
    在 App Store 或 Google Play 搜索 "Gmail"Search "Gmail" in the App Store or Google Play

    点击「创建账户」Tap "Create account"

    8 位以上 · 包含字母和数字 · 保存在安全的地方8+ characters with letters and numbers — keep it somewhere safe

    用于找回密码 — Google 会发短信验证码For password recovery — Google will text a code

在 Indeed 上找工作 Find a Job on Indeed

王太太想找餐厅工作 — 哪个搜索词最好？ Mrs. Wang wants restaurant work — which search term works best?
三个选项Three Options
A
"找工作"（中文搜索）"找工作" (Chinese search)
B
"job"（只一个字）"job" (one word)
C
"server" 或 "cook" + 城市"server" or "cook" + city
为什么Why
Indeed 主要是英文 — 中文搜不到多少结果Indeed is mostly English — Chinese returns few results
太广 — 会出来几万个不相关的工作Too broad — tens of thousands of unrelated jobs
具体职位 + 城市 = 最相关的结果Specific role + city = most relevant results
✓ 正确答案：C✓ Correct Answer: C
具体职位 + 城市 = 找到合适的工作。可以试："server", "cook", "dishwasher", "cashier", "warehouse worker", "driver", "caregiver" + Los Angeles。 Specific role + city finds the right jobs. Try: "server", "cook", "dishwasher", "cashier", "warehouse worker", "driver", "caregiver" + Los Angeles.

申请工作Applying

填写在线申请表 Filling Out Online Applications

大部分申请表都问这四类问题 — 提前准备好答案 Most applications ask these four sections — prep your answers ahead
  • 1
    个人信息Personal Information
    姓名 · 地址 · 电话 · 电子邮件 · SSN 或 ITIN（如适用）Name · address · phone · email · SSN or ITIN (if asked)
  • 2
    工作经验Work History
    公司名 · 职位 · 起止年月 · 主要职责 · 上司姓名Company · job title · start/end dates · duties · supervisor's name
  • 3
    教育背景Education
    最高学历即可 · 没有学位写「High School」或「N/A」Highest level only — write "High School" or "N/A" if no degree
  • 4
    推荐人 (References)References
    2–3 位前上司或同事 · 提前问过他们 · 准备姓名、电话、电邮2–3 former bosses or coworkers — ask them first, then list name, phone, email
  • 5
    提交前再看一遍Re-read before you submit
    电话号、邮件、日期 — 这三个最常填错Phone, email, dates — these are the most common typos

申请工作Applying

申请表上不要写什么 What NOT to Put

在中国常见 — 在美国不需要、甚至会让你被淘汰 Common in China — unnecessary or harmful on US applications
✓ 写这些Include
  • 姓名（真名）Real name
  • 联系电话和电邮Phone and email
  • 工作经验Work experience
  • 语言能力（中、英、粤）Languages (Chinese, English, Cantonese)
  • 是否有合法工作身份Authorization to work (yes/no)
✗ 不要写Don't Include
  • 个人照片Personal photo— US 不需要 · 可能被认为偏见— not used in US, can lead to bias
  • 年龄或生日Age or birthday
  • 婚姻状况、孩子Marital status, children
  • 宗教信仰Religion
  • SSN（除非已收到 offer）SSN (only after offer)
💡 雇主如果在面试前问这些 — 可以礼貌地说「我希望面试后再讨论」。💡 If asked these before an interview, you can politely say "I'd prefer to discuss after we meet."
